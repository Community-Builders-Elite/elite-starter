# research

This is your scratchpad. It holds dated notes and things you're still looking into. Messy is fine here, that's the point.

## What goes here

- A competitor you studied
- An idea you're chewing on
- Notes from something you read or watched
- Anything you're researching but haven't decided on yet

## What does NOT go here

- Settled facts about your business (those go in `reference`)
- Finished content (that goes in `outputs`)

## How to use it

When you're exploring something, tell Claude "save this to research." It files it here, usually with the date in the name so you can see when you looked into it.

If something you researched turns out to be important and permanent, tell Claude to "move this to reference." That's how a rough note becomes settled truth.

The `_master-index.md` file is the table of contents for this folder. Claude keeps it updated.
