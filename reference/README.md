# reference

This is your library. It holds the always-true facts about your business: the things that don't change very often.

## What goes here

- Your offer and pricing
- Who your audience is
- Your voice and writing style
- Testimonials and proof
- Anything you want Claude to treat as settled truth

## What does NOT go here

- Things you're still figuring out (those go in `research`)
- Finished content Claude made for you (that goes in `outputs`)

## How to use it

You don't have to organize this by hand. When you tell Claude something worth keeping, say "save this to my reference," and it files it here and updates the index.

The `_master-index.md` file is the table of contents for this folder. Claude keeps it updated so you can always find what's in here.
