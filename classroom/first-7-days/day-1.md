## A quick word on "repos"

You're going to hear the word "repo" a lot. Don't let it trip you up. A repo is just a project folder. The full word is "repository," but in plain terms it's a folder that holds all your files and can be backed up online. This kit is a repo. Your copy of it will be your repo.

Why your own copy? Because the original starter kit is shared. If everyone worked in the same one, it would be chaos. So you make your own copy, with your name on it, and you can change anything you want without touching the original. You literally cannot break the original. That's the whole point.
