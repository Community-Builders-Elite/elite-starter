# Day 2: Tell Claude About Your Business

Yesterday you got your own workspace. Today we make Claude actually useful by teaching it who you are.

## Why this is the most important day

Here's the thing that makes Claude feel like magic instead of a generic robot: it can sound like YOU. Not like a corporate brochure, not like every other AI post you've scrolled past, but like you, talking to your people, in your voice.

But it can't do that until it knows you. That's what today is for.

## Meet your "business brain"

In your repo there's a file called CLAUDE.md. Think of it as your business brain. Every single time you open this folder and start working, Claude reads this file automatically. Whatever's in it, Claude knows.

So if your business brain says "I'm a fitness coach who helps busy moms, I talk warm and direct, I never use hype," then every post, email, and message Claude writes for you will reflect that. The better this file is, the better everything else gets. It's the foundation the whole kit is built on.

## You don't fill it in alone

You could open the file and type your answers in by hand. But there's an easier way, and it's the way we recommend.

There's a helper called /setup-coach. You type it, and Claude interviews you. It asks a handful of short, friendly questions about your business: who you help, what you sell, your big promise, how you talk, and any rules you want Claude to always follow. You answer in your own words, even rough answers are fine, and Claude writes the whole thing up for you and saves it.

It takes about ten minutes. If you already have a document about your business, you can paste that in instead and Claude will organize it for you.

## What to actually answer

Don't overthink it. Answer like you'd explain your business to a friend at a coffee shop. The questions are:

- What's your business and who do you help?
- What do you sell, and what does it cost?
- What's the big result you help people get?
- How do you talk and write? Any phrases you use a lot?
- Anything you NEVER want Claude to do?
- Do you run a community? (If not, just say no.)

## Your action in Claude Code

Open your repo, start Claude, and say:

> "Let's do Day 2."

Claude will kick off the interview (or help you paste in what you have). Just answer the questions.

## Your win for today

When you're done, test it. Ask Claude to write something small, like a one-line bio or a quick intro post. Watch it use what you just told it. That moment, when it sounds like you, is when it clicks.

Your business brain is alive now. Tomorrow we make sure your work never gets lost.
