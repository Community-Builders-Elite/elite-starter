# outputs

This is your finished work. Anything Claude makes for you that's done lands here.

## What goes here

- Posts, emails, and captions
- Plans and outlines
- Any content that's ready to go out into the world

## What does NOT go here

- Settled facts about your business (those go in `reference`)
- Notes you're still working through (those go in `research`)

## How to use it

When Claude finishes making something for you, it saves it here so you always have a record of your work. You can ask "show me what we made last week" and Claude can look here.

You can organize this however you like as you grow. A simple start is a folder per type, like `posts`, `emails`, and `plans`. Claude can set those up when you need them.

The `_master-index.md` file is the table of contents for this folder. Claude keeps it updated.
